Badminton Skill Challenge Sheet

Work with your partner to complete the five challenges below. Record each attempt on video and have the instructor verify completion.

Challenge 1: Clear to Backcourt

Goal

Hit the shuttle high from the backcourt so it travels over the opponent’s head and lands in the opponent’s rear court.

Instructions

Stand near opposite baselines with your partner. Succeed 5 consecutive times to pass.

Challenge 2: Net Drop

Goal

Control your touch so the shuttle softly passes over the net and lands in the frontcourt.

Instructions

Stand near the net with your partner. Land the shuttle within 1 meter of the net 5 times in a row to pass.

Challenge 3: Smash

Goal

Learn the overhead swing and downward hitting for a smash.

Instructions

Your partner tosses the shuttle above your head. Jump and hit it downward powerfully. Succeed 3 times to pass.

Challenge 4: Basic Rally

Goal

Develop basic returning ability and coordination.

Instructions

Rally with light force near mid-court. Keep the shuttle going back and forth for 10 consecutive hits without dropping to pass.

Challenge 5: Star Footwork

Goal

Get familiar with moving to each area of the badminton court.

Instructions

From the center, move to eight directions and return to the center. Finish one full cycle within 60 seconds to pass.
